# Open Questions

Critical unknowns to resolve during research. These are **cross-cutting** — affect multiple countries. Per-country findings populate individual `countries/*.md` Block 8.

## OQ-1: Polish karta pobytu interaction
- Does holding a Polish residence permit block applying for temporary protection elsewhere?
- Is there an EU-wide "one country of temporary protection" rule? (See EU TP Directive Art. 9.)
- Can one surrender Polish status and re-register in another country?
- Does the Polish KP type matter (humanitarian, work, family)?
- **Affects**: all EU/Schengen countries §5.1.

## OQ-2: Partner status for family reunification
- Is cohabitation / partnership recognized for non-EU nationals, or is marriage required?
- How does this vary by country?
- What documents prove cohabitation in countries that accept it?
- **Affects**: all countries §5.9.

## OQ-3: Remote IT tax regimes
- Can a Ukrainian IT worker earning ~$3000/mo from foreign clients register as self-employed / freelancer / sole entrepreneur in each country?
- Special "digital nomad" tax regimes per country?
- Effective monthly tax burden after all contributions?
- Specific IT/HQE talent programmes?
- **Affects**: all countries §5.3.

## OQ-4: Student status of Ukrainian university student abroad
- Does enrolment in a Ukrainian uni (distance learning) qualify for any student residence permit abroad?
- Can she apply for a student visa in a new country while still enrolled in Ukraine?
- Does her lack of income block family reunification or dependent status?
- **Affects**: all countries §5.9.

## OQ-5: Post-4-March-2027 transitional mechanisms
- Which countries have announced concrete pathways from TP to regular residence?
- Does time under TP count toward PR / citizenship?
- What's the realistic risk of no announcement before deadline?
- Are there fallback paths (DN-visa, freelance permit) accessible before 03.2027?
- **Affects**: all countries §5.1 (post-2027 part).

## OQ-6: Minimum income thresholds for couples
- Required minimum income to support a non-working partner in each country.
- Is $3000/mo sufficient for the sponsoring partner across DN/family/self-employed paths?
- How does marriage change the threshold (often lower for spouse than partner)?
- **Affects**: all countries §5.1, §5.9.

# Open Questions

## Critical unknowns to resolve during research

1. **Impact of Polish karta pobytu on temporary protection in other EU states**
   - Does holding Polish residence permit block applying for temporary protection elsewhere?
   - Is there an EU-wide "one country of temporary protection" rule?
   - Can one surrender Polish status and re-register in another country?

2. **Definition of "couple" for family reunification across EU**
   - Is cohabitation / partnership recognized, or is marriage required?
   - How does this vary by country for non-EU nationals?

3. **Remote work tax regimes in each candidate country**
   - Can a Ukrainian IT worker earning $3000/mo from foreign clients register as self-employed / freelancer in each country?
   - What are the actual monthly tax burdens after all contributions?
   - Are there special regimes for "digital nomads" that the IT worker could use?

4. **Student status of Ukrainian university student abroad**
   - Does being enrolled in a Ukrainian university (distance learning) qualify for any student residence permit abroad?
   - Can she apply for a student visa in a new country while still enrolled in Ukraine?
   - Does her lack of income block family reunification or dependent status?

5. **Post-March 2027 transitional mechanisms**
   - Have any countries announced concrete pathways from temporary protection to regular residence?
   - Does time spent under temporary protection count toward permanent residence / citizenship?

6. **Minimum income thresholds for residence permits for couples**
   - What is the required minimum income to support a non-working partner in each country?
   - Is $3000/mo sufficient for the sponsoring partner?

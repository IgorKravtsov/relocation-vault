## Cross-country observations

- Currency risk is not the lead differentiator across these four because all are euro-area destinations; the practical financial risk is USD-to-EUR conversion plus local banking/KYC and self-employed tax registration.
- Ukraine-posture is acceptable for screening in all four because each has captured Ukraine reception / TP evidence, but the post-2027 ordinary-status bridge remains country-specific and should not be assumed from general support.
- Travel ties to Ukraine are strongest for Greece among this subset only in relative geography; Portugal and Spain need larger travel buffers, while Italy depends heavily on chosen city.
- Adaptation risk is lowest in Portugal because of the captured English-proficiency signal, medium in Spain/Greece, and higher in Italy because the profile already needs Italian-speaking support for leases, healthcare, tax/accounting, and immigration paperwork.
